About Us

Empower students to thrive academically by providing convenient, affordable, and high-quality supplies and resources tailored to their diverse needs, fostering success throughout their educational journey. Dylan, Meri, Adry, and Chloe, students at Michigan State University, founded Campus Crates to simplify college life. Recognizing the hassle of getting supplies and textbooks, they launched a subscription service delivering essentials to students' doorsteps. With passion and teamwork, they quickly expanded, aiming to revolutionize the college experience nationwide.

FAQ

  • Q: What is Campus Crates?
    A: Campus Crates is a subscription service designed to provide college students with essential supplies, textbooks, and other resources tailored to their academic needs. We aim to simplify the college experience by delivering these items directly to students' doorsteps, saving them time and hassle.
  • Q: How does Campus Crates work?
    A: To get started with Campus Crates, simply sign up for a subscription plan on our website. You can choose from a variety of package options based on your academic interests and preferences. Once subscribed, you'll receive a curated selection of supplies and resources delivered to you at regular intervals throughout the semester.
  • Q: Can I cancel or modify my Campus Crates subscription?
    A: Yes, you can manage your subscription through your account on our website. You can cancel, pause, or modify your subscription at any time before the next billing cycle. We strive to make the process as convenient as possible for our customers.
